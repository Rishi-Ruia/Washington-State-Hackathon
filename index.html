What is this?

A 48-hour hackathon for high school students across Washington State. Build something meaningful, learn new skills, and connect with other student developers.

Whether you're coding your first website or building complex applications, this event is designed for all skill levels.

Open to All

Any Washington high school student can participate, regardless of experience level.

The Challenge

Challenge will be revealed when the event starts

Build an app or website that can have a meaningful impact on a community you're part of.

What counts as a community?

Your School

Help students find study groups or organize events

Your Neighborhood

Connect neighbors or share local resources

Cultural Community

Celebrate heritage or connect cultural groups

Interest Group

Support gamers, artists, athletes, or musicians

Example Projects

  • • Tutoring matcher for students and peer tutors
  • • Community garden planner for shared spaces
  • • Tournament organizer for school esports
