What is The Washington State Hackathon?

For All WA Students

Open to all high school students in Washington State. Whether you're a coding pro or complete beginner, you're welcome here!

Solo or Team

Work independently or team up with a friend! Teams can have a maximum of 2 members. Solo projects are equally welcome.

Build & Learn

Create something meaningful while developing your skills. This is about impact, creativity, and personal growth!

The Challenge

Challenge Locked

The prompt will be revealed when the event starts!

Build an app or website that can have a meaningful impact on a community you're part of.

AI Tools Welcome!

AI tools like ChatGPT, Claude, and GitHub Copilot are allowed and encouraged. Use whatever helps you build!

What counts as a community?

Your School

Help students find study groups, organize events, or improve campus life.

Your Neighborhood

Connect neighbors, share local resources, or address community needs.

Cultural Community

Celebrate heritage, share language resources, or connect with cultural groups.

Hobby or Interest Group

Support gamers, artists, athletes, musicians, or any group you're passionate about.

What to Submit

Required

Video or PowerPoint

Create a short video demo OR PowerPoint presentation showing your project in action. Keep it simple and focused!

Required

Written Explanation

Explain what your project does and why it matters. What problem does it solve?

Optional

Live Link

If you deployed your website or app online, share the link! Not required, but encouraged if possible.
